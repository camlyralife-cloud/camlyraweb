Who We Are

Science-backed care. Human at its core.

Calmyra was founded on the belief that the highest-performing people — and the people who love them — deserve mental health support that matches the complexity of their lives.

We are a premium mental wellness practice headquartered in Dubai, with a South Asia centre in Bangalore and an online practice serving clients worldwide. Our team of psychologists, counsellors, and coaches brings together professional expertise and genuine warmth — so every session moves you forward, not just through it.

Whether you are navigating anxiety, rebuilding after trauma, working through a relationship challenge, or ready to architect a life that truly fits you — we are here, and we are qualified.

Why Calmyra

Care refined by experience. Delivered with precision.

Qualified Professionals

Our India team holds postgraduate clinical qualifications and is RCI registered. Our Dubai coaches and consultants are qualified practitioners in their respective disciplines. You are always working with someone properly trained.

Integrated, Not Fragmented

We do not separate clinical care from coaching. For clients who need both, our teams coordinate — so your therapist and coach share a unified picture of your goals and your progress.

Genuinely Personalised

Your intake, care plan, therapist match, and session format are all designed around you — not around what is easiest to schedule. If your needs evolve, your plan evolves with them.

Absolute Discretion

In Dubai, privacy is not a preference — it is a requirement. Our client records, session content, and billing are completely confidential. We do not reference our clients in any form without explicit written consent.

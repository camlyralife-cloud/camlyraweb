Who We Are

Science-backed care. Human at its core.

Calmyra was built on a single conviction: that people who are serious about their wellbeing deserve professionals who are equally serious about delivering it. Not wellness generalisms. Not motivational scripts. Actual expertise, rigorously applied.

In Bangalore, our registered psychologists and counsellors — each holding postgraduate clinical qualifications — deliver evidence-based psychological support for individuals, couples, children, and families navigating everything from anxiety and trauma to relationship challenges and life transitions.

In Dubai, our qualified coaches and consultants work with professionals, leaders, and families in a developmental, forward-focused model — building the clarity, mindset, and performance that high-functioning lives demand. Our Dubai programmes are non-clinical and entirely growth-oriented.

Whether you are in Bangalore, Dubai, or anywhere in the world, your initial consultation is entirely complimentary. No obligation. No pressure. Just a conversation to understand where you are and where you want to be.

Why Calmyra

Care refined by experience. Delivered with precision.

Qualified Professionals

In India, our team holds postgraduate clinical qualifications and is registered with the relevant statutory bodies governing psychological practice. In Dubai, our coaches and consultants hold internationally recognised certifications and operate within a structured, supervised framework. You are always working with someone properly trained.

Integrated, Not Fragmented

We do not treat psychological care and coaching as separate worlds. For clients who benefit from both — counselling in India and developmental coaching for their global work — our teams share context and coordinate approach, so every part of your journey moves in the same direction.

Genuinely Personalised

Your intake process, care plan, practitioner match, and session format are designed around you — not around what is easiest to schedule. As your needs evolve, your plan evolves with them.

Absolute Discretion

Your records, session content, and identity are held in the strictest confidence. We do not share, publish, or reference client information in any form. Our privacy practices meet UAE data protection regulations and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

Your assessment journey

Every assessment follows the same thoughtful pathway — from first conversation to a care plan built around your findings.

  1. 01
    Initial Consultation (Complimentary · 20 minutes)

    A relaxed conversation with a clinical team member to understand what you are looking for and identify which assessment pathway is most relevant to your needs.

  2. 02
    Assessment Session

    An in-depth session using validated psychological instruments, conducted or directly supervised by a licensed psychologist.

  3. 03
    Clinical Review & Interpretation

    Your results are reviewed, interpreted, and contextualised. Never handed over as raw data without meaning. We make sense of it together.

  4. 04
    Feedback Session & Care Plan

    A dedicated session in which your results are explained clearly, your questions are answered fully, and a personalised care plan is built around your findings.

Assessment Types

📋

Patient-friendly pathways designed around what you are navigating — each interpreted by a licensed psychologist, never reduced to a score alone.

Mood & Emotional Wellbeing

For those navigating persistent low mood, anxiety, emotional swings, or burnout. Identifies patterns, severity, and contributing factors.

Clinical tools: PHQ-9 · Beck Depression Inventory · Hamilton Anxiety Scale

Personality & Behavioural Profile

Understand how you are wired — how you think under pressure, how you relate to others, how you make decisions.

Tools: Big Five · MBTI · Behavioural Functional Analysis

Cognitive Functioning

Evaluates memory, attention, processing speed, and executive function. Relevant for cognitive changes, burnout, or performance optimisation.

Tools: Raven's Progressive Matrices · Wechsler Adult Intelligence Scale

Relationship & Attachment

Maps your attachment style, communication patterns, and relational dynamics for individuals or couples.

Child & Developmental

Specialist evaluation for children and adolescents — developmental milestones, learning needs, behavioural patterns, and emotional wellbeing.

Tools: Denver Developmental Screening · DMIT

Executive Performance Profile

For leaders, founders, and senior professionals. Combines cognitive, personality, and stress-response profiling to identify performance limiters.

Tools: EQ-i Leadership Assessment · Cognitive Load Analysis · Stress & Resilience Profiling

All assessments are administered or directly supervised by licensed psychologists using internationally validated clinical instruments.

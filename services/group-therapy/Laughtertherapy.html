What is Laughter Therapy?

Laughter Therapy is a therapeutic technique that uses natural laughter to improve emotional, mental, and physical well-being.

It is based on the idea that laughter reduces stress, improves mood, and enhances overall health. It can be practiced through exercises, group sessions, or guided activities.

What It Helps With

  • Stress Reduction: Releases endorphins and reduces stress levels.
  • Mood Improvement: Helps reduce anxiety and depression.
  • Better Immunity: Strengthens immune response.
  • Pain Relief: Promotes natural relaxation and relief.
  • Social Connection: Improves bonding and relationships.

Basic Information

  • Sessions: 30–60 minutes group or individual sessions.
  • Techniques: Breathing, laughter exercises, and playful activities.
  • Settings: Hospitals, workplaces, wellness centers.
  • Benefits: Long-term emotional and physical well-being.
  • Who Can Join: Suitable for all age groups.

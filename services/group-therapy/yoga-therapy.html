What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that combines physical postures, breathing techniques, meditation, and ethical living for holistic well-being.

It helps harmonize the body, mind, and spirit, promoting strength, flexibility, emotional balance, and inner peace.

What It Helps With

  • Stress Reduction: Calms the nervous system and reduces anxiety.
  • Flexibility & Strength: Improves muscle tone and mobility.
  • Posture Improvement: Supports spinal alignment and reduces pain.
  • Mental Clarity: Enhances focus and mindfulness.
  • Emotional Balance: Promotes relaxation and positivity.

Basic Information

  • Sessions: 30–90 minutes individual or group practice.
  • Styles: Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Yin, Bikram.
  • Settings: Studios, hospitals, workplaces, home practice.
  • Benefits: Physical, mental, and emotional wellness.
  • Who Can Join: Suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

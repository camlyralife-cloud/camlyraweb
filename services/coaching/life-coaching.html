Designed for the life you want. Grounded in the science of how to get there.

Calmyra's life coaching is not motivational speaking. It is a structured, psychology-backed process for the person who is already capable of more — and ready to prove it.

What life coaching is (and is not)

Life coaching and therapy serve different purposes, and at Calmyra we are always transparent about the distinction.

Therapy works with the past — healing wounds, resolving trauma, treating mental health conditions. Life coaching works with the present and the future — building clarity, dismantling self-imposed limits, and constructing a path that is genuinely yours.

What makes our coaching different is that it is grounded in psychology. Our coaches draw on established frameworks — values-clarification, behavioural science, cognitive reframing, solution-focused methodology — so every insight has a mechanism behind it, not just a feeling.

Who it is for

  • Professionals at a career crossroads — seeking clarity on what comes next
  • Entrepreneurs building businesses while trying to preserve their identity outside work
  • Expatriates in Dubai creating an intentional life far from their original context
  • Individuals in major life transitions — relocation, relationship change, milestone birthday
  • High-achievers who are succeeding externally but feel privately unfulfilled

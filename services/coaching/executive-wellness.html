Executive Wellness

The pressure at the top is unlike anything else. The care should be too.

Calmyra's Executive Wellness Programme is a confidential, clinical-grade mental health and performance service designed specifically for leaders, founders, and senior professionals in the UAE.

Request a Confidential Consultation

Why leaders need dedicated care

Leadership carries a weight that is rarely visible and almost never acknowledged. The expectation to be decisive, composed, and certain — regardless of what is actually happening inside — creates a specific form of psychological pressure that standard wellness programmes were never designed for.

Add to this the particular experience of Dubai: a city built on velocity, where ambition is currency, identity is tied to achievement, and the expectation of resilience is total. The result is a population of highly capable people who are quietly, and sometimes seriously, struggling — with no obvious or safe channel for it.

The Calmyra Executive Wellness Programme exists for exactly this person.

Programme Components

Clinical Psychological Assessment

Comprehensive profile — cognitive functioning, stress-response, personality dynamics, emotional regulation.

Individual Therapy (where indicated)

Confidential therapy with a licensed psychologist for burnout, anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or acute performance stress.

Performance & Leadership Coaching

Psychology-backed coaching for decision fatigue, leadership identity, delegation, presence under pressure, and sustainable high performance.

Sleep & Recovery Optimisation

Evidence-based intervention for sleep quality — the single most underestimated performance variable in executive life.

Quarterly Wellness Reviews

Structured check-ins to track progress and adjust the programme as circumstances evolve.

In-Person or Fully Remote

All services at our Dubai centre or via encrypted video. Scheduling designed around your commitments.
